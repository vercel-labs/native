feature: **Text alignment and grid columns are first-class options** (friction #84): `ElementOptions.text_alignment` (start|center|end, consumed by text leaves — plain and wrapped — status bars, and surface titles) and `ElementOptions.columns` (fixed grid column count; 0 keeps the derived near-square grid) replace the post-set workarounds, and markup gains the matching `text-alignment` and `columns` attributes in both engines, the validator, and the LSP. `columns` is grid-scoped in the validator (the only layout that reads it — same policy as `gap` on stacking containers), and integer attributes now flow through markup generally (literal or `{binding}`).
