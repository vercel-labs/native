feature: **Span scale and underline in Native markup**: `<span>` gains `scale` — a positive multiplier on the paragraph's base size (the text element's `size` rung included), so inline headings and hero stats no longer need a Zig view — and `underline`, the span model's underline decoration. Both take literals or `{bindings}` (scale bindings must produce a number), both engines lower them through the same span-paragraph builder, line breaking measures scaled runs at their scaled size, and the paragraph still announces as one text run.
- **Scale teaching errors**: zero, negative, and non-numeric multipliers fail with a pinned message in the validator and both engines — the engine would silently draw such a run at the base size, so markup refuses the dead value instead.
- The soundboard's album-detail heading — previously called out as a Zig-only section — converts to a markup span paragraph, held widget-identical to the builder form by test.
