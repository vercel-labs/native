feature: **Status-item popover (macOS)**: `TrayOptions.popover_window` (and `UiApp` `status_item.popover_window`) names an app window whose content anchors to the tray as a transient `NSPopover` — left-clicking the status button toggles the panel (the dropdown menu, when declared, moves to right-click), the window itself never shows as a normal window, and the SDK's own metal canvas keeps rendering the same view inside the popover.
- **Popover automation**: `Runtime.toggleTrayPopover()` and the reserved `native-sdk.tray.toggle-popover` native-command drive the popover without a real mouse; open/close reports arrive as the `tray.popover_opened` / `tray.popover_closed` commands (source `.tray`) and the snapshot's tray line carries `popover_window=` / `popover_visible=`.
