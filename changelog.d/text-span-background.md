feature: **Per-span text backgrounds**: `TextSpan` gains `background: ?TextSpanColor`, a color-token reference (same namespace as span `color`) that fills the run's full line-box rect behind the glyphs — intra-line diff emphasis can now tint just the changed region of a line. Backgrounds are purely visual: measurement and wrapping are untouched, adjacent same-background runs abut without seams, and a background change invalidates retained span state.
