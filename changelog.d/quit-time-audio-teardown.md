fix: **Quitting while audio plays no longer crashes on exit**: closing the last window with a track playing used to segfault during shutdown — main's deferred app deinit ran after the runner had already destroyed the platform host, and stopping the still-active playback called into freed memory. The runtime now guarantees the app's stop hook runs before its loop returns (on the platform's shutdown event, or from the loop's exit path when an error unwind skips it), the UiApp tears its effects channel down there — silencing audio, disarming timers, joining effect workers — while the platform is alive, and the channel severs its services binding so the late deinit answers inert. Same contract on macOS, Linux, and Windows; embed hosts forward the hook too.
