feature: **Audio playback**: a first-class audio seam end to end — `fx.playAudio` plays a local file through the platform's single audio player (macOS: AVAudioPlayer in the AppKit host), with `pauseAudio`/`resumeAudio`/`stopAudio`/`seekAudio`/`setAudioVolume` transport and every report (load acknowledgment with the real decoded duration, coarse ~500ms position ticks while playing, exactly-one completion, explicit failures) arriving as one `on_event` Msg (`EffectAudio`). Effects are the recorded boundary, so sessions journal the app's commands and the delivered events — replay stays deterministic without recording any audio. GTK/Win32 report `audio_playback` unsupported and every call degrades to one loud `.failed` Msg; the null platform ships a deterministic fake player (duration table, hand-advanced position) so suites stay hermetic. The automation snapshot reports playback honestly (`audio key=... state=... position_ms=...`).
