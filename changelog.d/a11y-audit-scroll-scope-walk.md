fix: **A11y focus-reachable audit forgives below-the-fold rows through outer clip scopes**: once the ancestor walk crosses a vertically scrolling scope (a scroll region or windowed virtual list), a widget's layout-space y is scroll content, not screen geometry — scrolling carries it into every outer scope's band. The audit used to re-flag every focusable row scrolled below the fold when a pane column clipped the scroll's frame (any long list in a clipping pane produced false `focus_unreachable` findings); the walk now carries the vertical-scroll exemption up, while a horizontal full-clip past the pane's edge still reports (vertical scrolling never brings it into view).
