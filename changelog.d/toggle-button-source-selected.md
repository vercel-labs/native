fix: **Model-driven toggle-button chips follow the source** (friction #81): `toggle_button` retained its pressed state unconditionally across rebuilds, so a model-driven exclusive chip group (`selected="{p == pref}"`) showed every chip ever pressed as active. A toggle-button whose SOURCE asserts `selected` — on this rebuild or the previous one — is now model-driven: the source wins over the retained toggle (including when the user presses the already-active chip), so exactly the model's selection is active. Toggle-buttons whose source never asserts `selected` stay uncontrolled and keep the retained multi-select behavior (formatting-bar chips), and every other control kind keeps its existing retained-state contract. The soundboard header's theme chips drop their plain-button workaround and are real toggle-buttons again.
