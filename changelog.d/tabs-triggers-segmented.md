fix: **Markup tab strips render the house treatment**: `<button>` children of `<tabs>` now lower to `segmented_control` widgets in both markup engines (the widget kind the Zig builder's tab strips are built on), so the active trigger lifts to the surface with a hairline instead of disappearing into the strip's wash — ghost buttons' selected fill was the same `surface_subtle` as the tabs container, making the active tab unreadable. Handlers ride the widget id, so existing `selected=`/`on-press` bindings are untouched, and `toggle-button` children keep their kind.
