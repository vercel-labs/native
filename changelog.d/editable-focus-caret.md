fix: **Clicking an editable text widget now shows its caret and focus ring**: pointer focus kept `focus_visible` cleared for every widget, so a clicked field rendered no focus affordances at all — buttons and rows keep the quiet pointer focus, but editable text kinds now render ring and caret however focus arrived, the `:focus-visible` contract text inputs have on every platform.
- **Empty fields get a caret on click**: the pointer-selection path short-circuited when the computed selection equaled the implied default, so a click into an empty field (or past the end of the text) never stored a selection and the emitters drew no caret line.
- **Tab into a field places a caret**: keyboard focus landing on an editable without a selection now collapses one at the end of the text instead of rendering a ring with no insertion point.
