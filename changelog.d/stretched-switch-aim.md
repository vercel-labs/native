fix: **Automation clicks aim at the rendered control, not the stretched box**: a `switch_control` as a bare column child stretches to the full content width while its track renders ~42px at the left edge, so `widget-click`'s synthesized pointer at the frame's geometric center could land far from the visible control — or under an overlapping later-painted sibling — and silently miss. Pointer synthesis now aims at the control glyph's center for selection controls (switch/toggle track, checkbox box, radio circle) via the new shared `canvas.widgetControlAimPoint`; everything else keeps the frame center. `widget-action toggle` was already geometry-free (focus + space through the real keyboard path) and is regression-locked at both default and stretched layouts, including the covered-center shape that reproduces the live miss.
