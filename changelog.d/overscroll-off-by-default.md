improvement: **Overscroll is off by default**: scroll regions pin at their content edges on both scroll paths — the native macOS scroller gets non-elastic edges and the engine's wheel/kinetic physics clamp, with kinetic motion stopping cleanly at the boundary. Rubber-band bounce is a per-region opt-in (`overscroll="rubber_band"` in markup, `ElementOptions.overscroll = .rubber_band` in Zig views), and the `ScrollPhysics.overscroll` design token flips the app-wide default that per-region values override.
