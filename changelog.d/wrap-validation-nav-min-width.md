improvement: **`wrap` is loud where it is inert, and nav panes declare their own floor.** `ElementOptions.wrap` is text-leaf word wrapping only — rows and columns never flow-wrap their children, which is the layout system's design (one axis, no reflow) — but the option on a container was silently ignored and shipped with comments asserting wrapping that never happened. Markup now rejects `wrap` on anything but `<text>` with a teaching error (validator, interpreter, and compiled engine in parity, same policy as `gap` on stacking containers), and the Zig builder warns in Debug without ever failing the build (shipped apps carry the mistake). And `NavOptions` gains `min_width`: split panes clamp against the pane ROOT's `min_size`, so a chat/detail pane rooted in `ui.nav` can declare its floor directly instead of stamping `widget.layout.min_size.width` post-build.
