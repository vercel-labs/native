feature: **`icon=` grows past buttons**: `ElementOptions.icon` / markup `icon="..."` now works on `toggle-button` (chips — and tab strips, whose children are toggle-buttons), `list-item` and `menu-item` (a leading icon slot drawn before the label, with intrinsic widths accounting via shared metrics), and `badge` (icon-only centered or icon-before-label). Timeline items take `icon="check"` as a vector indicator (`TimelineItemOptions.icon` / the markup attr), and the stepper's completed step wears the vector `check` instead of the ✓ text glyph — which the bundled face cannot render (see the tofu guard). Both engines in parity with the validator/LSP teaching errors updated (`icon` stays a closed literal vocabulary everywhere). Deliberately skipped: `segmented_control` (not markup-expressible by design, no icon demand), `TextSpan` inline icons and the remaining composite text channels (a span-model design of its own), and selection controls (`checkbox`/`radio`/`switch` — their control glyph IS the leading visual).
