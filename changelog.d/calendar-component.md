feature: **Calendar component**: a day-grid month picker (`<calendar>` in markup, `Ui.calendar` in Zig) with single, range, and multiple selection, prev/next month navigation, today and min/max bounds, and muted adjacent-month days — display-only like the rest of the library, so the app model owns the shown month and the selection. Dates ride attributes as `YYYY-MM-DD` and day presses inject the pressed `CalendarDate` into a bare `on-select` Msg.
